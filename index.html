Past works

    Bachelor project - Path Integral Monte Carlo and Hydrogen bond

    In this final year Bachelor project, Feynman's path integral formulation of quantum mechanics is applied to develop a C++ Monte Carlo numerical simulation of 1D quantum systems, notably the hydrogen bond between water molecules.

    • Path Integral Monte Carlo and Hydrogen bond [PDF, english]

    The project was carried out at Imperial College London during my BSc exchange year outside of EPFL.

    Swiss Matura - Relatività ristretta e sue applicazioni allo SwissFEL

    In this Maturaarbeit/travail de maturité/lavoro di maturità, the theory of special relativity is presented and discussed. The intended target audience is of high school students.

    • Relatività ristretta e sue applicazioni allo SwissFEL [PDF, italian]

    • Interactive 1D Lorentz boost [HTML5, italian]

    These notes were written at Liceo Cantonale di Lugano 2 in the last high school year.
