    I'm a PhD candidate in Quantum Computing at CEA, Université Paris-Saclay, interested in error-mitigated quantum simulations of high energy physics and condensed matter systems.

Past works

Computer-assisted two-loops renormalization of six-dimensional scalar effective field theories

In this Master thesis, computer algebra programs in FORM and Wolfram Language are written to automatically calculate and solve the renormalization group equations of various scalar effective field theories up to 2 loops.

ETH Zürich, April 2023

Path Integral Monte Carlo and Hydrogen bond

In this final year Bachelor project, Feynman's path integral formulation of quantum mechanics is applied to develop a C++ Monte Carlo numerical simulation of 1D quantum systems, notably the hydrogen bond between water molecules.

Imperial College London, May 2020
