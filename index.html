Past works

    Master Thesis
    Computer-assisted two-loops renormalization of six-dimensional scalar effective field theories

    In this Master thesis, computer algebra programs in FORM and Wolfram Language are written to automatically calculate and solve the renormalization group equations of various scalar effective field theories up to 2 loops.

    • Computer-assisted two-loops renormalization of six-dimensional scalar effective field theories [PDF, english]

    The thesis was carried out at ETH Zürich in the Theoretical Particle Physics Group.

    Master seminar
    Path integrals in quantum mechanics, Semiclassical approximation

    In these notes, a complete derivation, a discussion of their properties and a few applications are shown for the path integral formalism and the WKB approximation in quantum mechanics.

    • Path integrals in quantum mechanics, Semiclassical approximation [PDF, english]

    • Lecture slides [PDF, english]

    The proseminar was held at ETH Zürich during my MSc studies.

    Bachelor Thesis
    Path Integral Monte Carlo and Hydrogen bond

    In this final year Bachelor project, Feynman's path integral formulation of quantum mechanics is applied to develop a C++ Monte Carlo numerical simulation of 1D quantum systems, notably the hydrogen bond between water molecules.

    • Path Integral Monte Carlo and Hydrogen bond [PDF, english]

    The project was carried out at Imperial College London during my BSc exchange year outside of EPFL.

    Swiss Matura
    Relatività ristretta e sue applicazioni allo SwissFEL

    In this Maturaarbeit/travail de maturité/lavoro di maturità, the theory of special relativity is presented and discussed. The intended target audience is of high school students.

    • Relatività ristretta e sue applicazioni allo SwissFEL [PDF, italian]

    • Interactive 1D Lorentz boost [HTML5, italian]

    These notes were written at Liceo Cantonale di Lugano 2 in the last high school year.
